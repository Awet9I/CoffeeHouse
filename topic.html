McLaren 765LT Spider

More facts...

Its “LT” name refers to the fact that it’s a “longtail” version of the 720s model, follow this link to see a pictur, with wich it shares many components. The mid-engined 765LT is powered by a twin-turbocharged 4.0-liter V-8 that is also featured in its 720S sibling. While the LT also feeds the rear wheels through a seven-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission, its mill has been fortified with parts from the Senna. These upgrades help the Longtail produce 755 horsepower—45 ponies more than the 720S. Its noxious fumes and furious sounds that grow louder the harder its gas pedal is pressed are released through a set of high-mounted exhaust ports. We estimate that the 765LT will hit 60 mph in 2.4 seconds and charge from naught to 100 mph in just 5.4 ticks. The 765LT demonstrates the same preternatural track abilities and communicative feedback that makes the 720S so great. And our experience with that McLaren has proved the company knows how to tune a chassis and immerse drivers every time they get behind the wheel. The interior can be customized with a mix of soft-grain leather and faux-suede upholstery as well as myriad color combinations. However, the LT's interior has been optimized to reduce weight and boost performance. However, climbing in and out of the thing continues to be a pain in the ass and there's virtually no cargo space. 765LT is limited-production vehicle and only 765 units of the 765LT Spider will be produced in total.
